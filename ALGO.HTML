What it is

Programmed over the 22'-23' summer holidays, AlgoBot was designed to primarily trade in the FOREX marker using a range of tested trading strategies. Python was the sole programming language used to develop my bot, whereby I leveraged the OANDA trading platform to scrape candle stick data, and place trades. Coupled with writing my own libraries to facilitate the development of my program, the pandas, NymPy, and Requests libraries were all used.
